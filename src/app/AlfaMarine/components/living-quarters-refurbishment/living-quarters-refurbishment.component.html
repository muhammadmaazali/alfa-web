LIVING QUARTERS REFURBISHMENT

ALFA offers end-to-end solutions for the refurbishment, upgrade, repair, and conversion of living quarters to meet evolving operational needs across the marine, offshore oil & gas, renewable energy, and defense sectors. Our expertise ensures the transformation or maintenance of facilities with precision, quality, and adherence to global standards. These services can be performed dockside or on an offshore platform or vessel.

Comprehensive Services
We deliver fully integrated Lump Sum Turnkey (LSTK) services, handling every aspect of project execution, from initial assessments to final installations:

  • Inspection and Feasibility Studies: Comprehensive site surveys, condition assessments, and feasibility analyses
  • Engineering and Design: Advanced conceptualization, detailed engineering, and 3D modeling for optimized solutions.
  • Material Procurement and Construction: High-quality material supply, deconstruction, and meticulous construction processes.
  • Life Extension and Recertification: Recommendations for extending the operational life of facilities and ensuring compliance with industry standards.
  • Service and Maintenance: Ongoing support, spare parts, and regular upkeep.

Specialized Capabilities
Our expertise extends to architectural outfitting, HVAC & refrigeration, electrical, instrumentation, and piping systems. We handle both individual room outfitting and full-scale refurbishments for facilities, including:

  • Turnkey Conversions and Upgrades:
    • Service and Maintenance: Insulation: Thermal, acoustic, and fire-rated solutions for walls, ceilings, and floors.
    • Paneling: Lightweight, decorative, wet area, acoustic, and fire-rated options (B, A & H classifications).
    • Ceilings and Flooring: Fire-rated ceilings, decorative epoxy, vinyl, ceramic tiles, anti-slip safety flooring, and more.
    • Doors and Windows: Internal and external fire-rated installations (B, A & H classifications).
    • Furniture and Modular Units: Bespoke joinery, bathrooms units, and locker room setups.
    • Galley and Laundry Equipment: Efficient installations tailored to operational needs.
    • Soft Furnishings and Shading: Blackout blinds, curtains, and HDPE fire-resistant shading materials.
  • Living Quarters: Cabins, mess rooms, galleys, offices, recreation rooms, laundry, washrooms, corridors, and stairwells

Applications
