Blast-Rated Modules

Our blast-rated modules, manufactured in our KSA facility, are designed in accordance with API 752/753 recommended practices. These modules are engineered to withstand blast events with performance options:

  • Low Response • Low Response: Minimal repairs required post-blast, allowing immediate reuse.
  • Medium Response Widespread damage requiring significant repairs, but the building remains reusable.
  • High Response: Extensive damage where repair costs are equivalent to new construction.

Applications

Our fully rated and certified modules provide high-quality living and operational environments in challenging conditions. Key applications include:

  • Living Quarters: Accommodation modules, recreation facilities, galleys, messrooms, locker rooms, and offices.
