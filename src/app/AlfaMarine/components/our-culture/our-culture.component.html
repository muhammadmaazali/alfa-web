Our Culture

Building a Sustainable Future, Together We foster a culture that empowers our employees to develop novel solutions and be the best they can be. Our working environment is built on a foundation of mutual respect and support and driven by teamwork and innovation. Together, we are continually evolving to meet the demands of the industry and shaping the world of marine & offshore, construction and EPC for the better.

Culture and Values

ALFA is defined by a strong and cohesive company culture. We strive to cultivate a working environment where everyone can thrive. From maintaining a zero-tolerance approach to discrimination to providing boundless training and development opportunities, we take care of our people. We also endeavor to engage our employees in our core values, ensuring sustainability and responsibility is embedded into the day to day. Whether on-site, in the office, or in the boardroom, we live our purpose at every moment

For Our Community

We cultivate strong relationships with local partners and organizations, providing our employees with opportunities to give back and volunteer. We also prioritize the employment of KSA nationals, the use of local suppliers, and a wide range of additional measures designed to give back to the communities we operate in.

For Our People

Our people are critical to our success, so we take care to provide a wide range of learning and development opportunities to enable everyone to reach their full potential. At ALFA, everyone is given equal opportunities and respected for the unique background and perspective they bring to the team.

ALFA strategy

ALFA aims to offer differentiated, competitive products and solutions to the Energy Industry, generating sustainable value. Our strategy is focused on profitable, sustainable growth that would bring the utmost value to our stakeholders, shareholders, customers, and people.

Our strategy is structured around three pillars:

  • Business Growth
  • Differentiation (Innovation & Digital)
  • Transitioning into a Sustainable Future.
