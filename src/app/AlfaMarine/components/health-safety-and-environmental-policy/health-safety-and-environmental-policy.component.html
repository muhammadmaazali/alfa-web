HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENTAL (HSE) POLICY

ALFA Marine is dedicated to providing a safe and secure workplace for employees, clients, contractors, suppliers/vendors, and visitors across all facilities and project sites. We believe that through collaboration with clients and partners, occupational risks and environmental impacts can be effectively prevented. ALFA Marine is committed to protecting people, property, and communities, preventing pollution, and upholding the highest industrial standards for safety, health, and environmental sustainability in all its operations.

To achieve this, the Company pledges to:

  • Promote Awareness and Accountability
    • Ensure all employees and visitors understand their responsibilities regarding health, safety, and environmental protection.
    • Regularly measure and review their performance to maintain compliance and drive improvement.
  • Risk and Impact Management
    • Identify, assess, and mitigate health, safety, and environmental risks before commencing any operation or activity.
    • Continuously review and update risk management practices in line with applicable laws, regulations, and standards.
  • Clear Communication
    • Regularly communicate health, safety, and environmental objectives, policies, and procedures to employees and all stakeholders.
  • Training and Resource Provision
    • Provide employees with necessary education, resources, and training to ensure competence in their roles, including HSE accountability.
  • Best Practices in Facility Design
    • Design and construct facilities based on recognized best practices to safeguard people, property, and the environment.
  • Safe Facility Operations
    • Operate and maintain facilities in a manner that ensures the health and safety of all employees, clients, suppliers, contractors, and visitors.
  • HSE Adherence for Third Parties
    • Require contractors, suppliers, and visitors to adhere to the Company’s HSE policies, procedures, and objectives.
  • Emergency Preparedness
    • Maintain readiness for emergencies and respond promptly and effectively to incidents or accidents arising from project operations.
  • Incident Reporting and Review
    • Ensure all incidents are reported, investigated, and analyzed to drive continuous improvement and performance transparency.
  • Community Health and Safety Promotion
    • Foster awareness and practices that enhance health and safety beyond the workplace, supporting the community at large.
  • Continuous Improvement
    • Regularly review, analyze, and enhance the Company’s HSE programs and performance to ensure ongoing progress.

By adhering to these principles, ALFA Marine aims to achieve operational excellence while prioritizing the safety, health, and environmental sustainability of all its stakeholders.
